# Discover Corralejo — Design Phase 2

This update moves the interface closer to the approved wireframe and Varsha's visual direction.

## Welcome screen

- Full-screen Corralejo photography instead of a split hero/card layout.
- Larger, bolder Discover Corralejo title.
- Coral editorial accent line.
- Strong coral Start Tour button.
- Compact language control at the top-right.
- First-visit language selection opens as a polished bottom sheet.
- After choosing a language, the sheet closes and exposes the full-screen start experience.
- Change Language reopens the language sheet at any time.

## Shared design refinements

- Strong sans-serif headings closer to the Poppins-style wireframe.
- Less pill-shaped buttons and stronger rounded-rectangle calls to action.
- Stronger route overview title and hero hierarchy.
- Bolder stop-page titles and sticky walking controls.
- Existing Atlantic teal, warm sand, warm white, coral, ocean blue and ochre palette retained.

## Preserved functionality

- Eight languages.
- GA4 and consent handling.
- Offline download and service worker.
- All 18 stops and media.
- All 17 approved walking routes.
- Local PMTiles Corralejo map.

## Testing

After replacing the files, unregister the previous service worker, clear site data and reload. Test the welcome screen once with no saved language and once after returning from another page.
